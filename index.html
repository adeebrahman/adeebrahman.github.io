X-Maps




An accurate and responsive GIS with low learnability

User Interface




Search Options

X-Maps allows users to search from 4 different types of filters: Intersections , Streets and Points of Interests.

Choosing a Search Filter
  • Click on the "Search Options" drop down menu
  • Click on desired type of item to search
Searching for Intersections

When searching for an intersection the GIS will highlight and zoom in on the point if it exists and display the name of the intersection

Searching for Streets

When searching for a street the GIS will highlight the entire street


Searching for Points of Interest

When searching for a Point of Interest the GIS will draw a pin on all location. If only one such POI exists, the GIS will zoom to the map point

Finding Directions

1. Click on "Direction" button to expand search menu

2. Choose Start and Destination type
You have the option to find directions from Intersection -> Point Of Interest or Point of Interest -> Intersection and vice versa. Do what suits you best!

3. Choose Start and Destination location but either:

  • Clicking
    • or
  • Manually searching for Point of Interest/Intersection through the search bar
The starting location will be highlighted on the map with a white flag while the destination will be shown with a red flag

4. Click on "Get Path" for directions to be displayed
On the bottom right corner, detailed road instructions can be found. Click on "Get Path" again to clear path

Points of Interest

Zoom in on the GIS to interact with different types of Points of Interest, including:

  • Food locations
  • Educational institutions
  • Emergency services
  • Entertainment options
  • Financial establishments and
  • Health Centers
Click on the "Points of Interest" drop down menu and click on the checkboxes to filter out the options or turn off the feature completely.

Accessibility Settings

To make X-maps usable for everyone dispite any disability, multiple color schemes are available in the GIS.

To access these color schemes:

  • Click on the "Accessibilty Settings" drop down menu
  • Click desired color settings

Default
Red-Green
Blue-Yellow
Monochrome
